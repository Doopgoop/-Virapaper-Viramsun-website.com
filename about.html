About Us

VIRAPAPERS is the Paper arm of the well-reputed Viramsun Nigeria Limited company, We are a leading Paper Conversion & Paper Distribution company in Nigeria, with a presence in West Africa since 1971. The Company began trading in paper, converting, and manufacturing exercise books in the mid-1970s.

VIRAPAPERS is today a leading supplier of several categories of high-quality paper and paper-related products (including paper raw materials) from Brazil, Europe, India, Indonesia, Singapore, and Thailand. The Company has established itself as the preferred distribution partner in Nigeria to several mills across a wide array of products.

Our goal is to deliver quality paper and packaging products on a consistent basis to our wide network of customers. With branch locations in Lagos, Kano, and Port Harcourt, we ensure that we meet the requirements of our entire customer base, including paper merchants, printing and publishing houses, stationery stores, wholesale packaging converters, corporate institutions, and government institutions, on time.
