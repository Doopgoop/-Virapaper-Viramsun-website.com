About VIRA Papers

VIRA Papers is a leading paper manufacturer and distributor in Nigeria. We specialize in high-quality paper products for schools, offices, and industries. With decades of experience, our mission is to provide sustainable and reliable paper solutions.

VIRAPAPERS is the Paper arm of the well-reputed Viramsun Nigeria Limited. We are a leading Paper Conversion & Distribution company in Nigeria, with a presence in West Africa since 1971, delivering quality paper and packaging products on a consistent basis.
